Frenzy

Frenzies run for 30 seconds at a time, giving you the opportunity to gain mass amounts of money based off your progress and in-game multipliers.*



*Currently not obtainable in-game
Discord Integration

With a built-in Discord Rich Presence, you can show off your progress to your friends and other players.

Features include:

  • Money Earned - Shows how much money you've earned in the game.
  • Money Per Second - Shows how much money you're earning per second.
  • Game State - Shows what you're currently doing in the game.

Achievements

With over 50 achievements to unlock, you'll have to work hard to get them all.

Achievements include:

  • Money Earned - Earn a certain amount of money.
  • Buying Shop Items - Earn a certain amount of money per second.
  • Game State - Discovering new features in the game.

Shop

With over 20 items to buy, you'll be able to spend your money on a variety of different things.

Auto-Save

Your data is automatically saved every 100 milliseconds, so you don't have to worry about losing your progress.


This progress is also exportable/importable.*


*exporting and importing is in the game, just not publicly yet.

Settings

With over 10 settings to change, you can customize your experience to your liking.


Settings include:

  • Theme - Choose between light and dark mode.
  • Auto-Save - Toggle auto-save on or off.
  • Discord Rich Presence - Toggle Discord Rich Presence on or off and change settings.

...and so much more!

There are so many more features that you'll have to discover for yourself!
